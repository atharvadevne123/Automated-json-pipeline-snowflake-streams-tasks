# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviour includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behaviour includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Discriminatory jokes or language
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behaviour may be reported by opening a GitHub issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All complaints will be reviewed and may result in a temporary or permanent ban.
